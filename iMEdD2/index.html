iMEdD one year anniversary: 24hour focus on journalism.

Posted on January 23, 2020January 24, 2020 | Announcements, News
REGISTER

9am Friday 07/02 – 9am Saturday 08/02

iMEdD – incubator for media education and development, celebrates its one-year operation anniversary with a 24hour event dedicated to journalism and its people.

Discussions with significant representatives of the industry from Greece and abroad, workshops focused on modern practices and tools, the long-awaited press première of Yorgos Avgeropoulos’ documentary “Agora II – Chained”, various parallel events and lots of music, will be the main ingredients of this year’s 24hour meeting of iMEdD.

In 2019, more than 20 programs were implemented in the context of the 4 active pillars: Incubator, Ideas Zone, Lab & Bridge, with more than 100 lecturers, mentors and associates and over 800 participants getting together in order to promote and strengthen transparency, credibility and independence in journalism. iMEdD, a non-profit journalism organisation established by the founding donation of Stavros Niarchos Foundation (SNF), completes its first year of operation, looking forward to a new, even more productive year.
